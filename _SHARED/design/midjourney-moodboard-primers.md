# Nendō · Moodboard Priming Prompts
> **Purpose:** 10 generalised world-building images to seed the Midjourney moodboard before generating the 10 production paintings
> **Method:** Run each prompt → favourite the best generation → add to your "Nendō" moodboard collection
> **Version:** 1.0 · March 2026

---

## Why Prime First

The existing 4 mood boards (overall dojo, lantern warmth, distant village, wooden floor) establish interior tone. These 10 additional primers expand the visual vocabulary outward — the world the dojo sits in, daytime light registers, elemental forces in nature, and the emotional light shifts the production paintings need to express. Once all 14 are in the moodboard, every subsequent `/imagine` inherits this richer palette.

## The 10 Primers

### P1 · Mountain Temple at Dusk
> Establishes the dojo as a real place in a real world. Gives Midjourney context for the architectural language — heavy timber, deep eaves, stone steps.

```
A small traditional Japanese temple perched on a wooded mountainside
at dusk, warm golden light from within glowing through paper screens,
dark cedar trees surrounding, mist curling between the trunks, stone
lantern on the path below, hand-painted watercolour style, Studio
Ghibli, deep peaceful solitude, warm and inviting
--ar 16:9 --s 750 --style raw --no text watermark characters people modern
```

### P2 · Dojo Courtyard in Soft Afternoon Light
> The dojo world in daytime. Proves this place exists under the sun — not only at night. Warm, golden, safe. Primes Student Profile and any lighter screens.

```
A quiet traditional Japanese dojo courtyard in soft afternoon sunlight,
warm golden light falling across raked gravel and moss-covered stones,
a wooden engawa veranda in shade, paper screens half-open showing a
dark cool interior beyond, dappled leaf shadows on warm wood, hand-painted
watercolour style, Studio Ghibli, lazy afternoon warmth, safe and
familiar, a place you want to sit and stay
--ar 16:9 --s 750 --style raw --no text watermark characters people modern
```

### P3 · Sunlit Forest Canopy (Overhead Green)
> Full daylight through trees — lush, alive, breathing. Counterbalances all the candlelit interiors. Seeds the Chi earth palette in its living form and gives the moodboard a green/gold register it currently lacks.

```
Looking upward through a dense Japanese forest canopy in bright
daylight, sunlight filtering through layers of green leaves creating
a mosaic of warm light and cool shadow, rich greens and soft gold,
the feeling of being small under ancient trees, hand-painted
watercolour style, Studio Ghibli, alive and breathing, warm green
light, dappled sunshine
--ar 16:9 --s 750 --style raw --no text watermark characters people buildings
```

### P8 · Shoji Screen Light Patterns
> The characteristic light quality of the dojo world — diffused, warm, directional through paper. Critical for every interior painting.

```

```

### P9 · Stone Lantern Path at Night
> The journey/threshold metaphor. The approach before arrival. Bridges the student profile (outside) and the dojo (inside).

```
A winding stone path through a Japanese garden at night, small stone
lanterns glowing softly along the path, dark moss and shaped shrubs

```

### P10 · Dawn Breaking Over Mountains
> The emotional resolution. After practice, the world opens up. Primes the session-complete painting with its warm dawn palette.

```
First light of dawn breaking over layered Japanese mountain ridges,
warm golden-pink light spreading across a deep indigo sky, the
horizon glowing with soft warmth, mist in the valleys below catching
the first light, a feeling of peaceful accomplishment and new
beginning, hand-painted watercolour style, Studio Ghibli, hopeful dawn
--ar 16:9 --s 750 --style raw --no text watermark characters people buildings
```

## Generation Order (Suggested Workflow)

Run in this order to build the moodboard in natural progression — exterior world → elemental forces → light quality → emotional arc:

| Order | Primer | What It Seeds |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | P1 · Mountain Temple at Dusk | Architectural language, the dojo's place in the world |
| 2 | P2 · Dojo Courtyard Afternoon | **DAYTIME** — warm golden light, safe/familiar register |
| 3 | P3 · Sunlit Forest Canopy | **DAYTIME** — green/gold overhead light, living world |
| 4 | P9 · Stone Lantern Path | Journey metaphor, student profile → dojo bridge |
| 5 | P8 · Shoji Screen Light | Interior light quality for all dojo paintings |
| 6 | P4 · Bamboo / Kaze | Wind element nature reference |
| 7 | P6 · Autumn Floor / Chi | Earth element nature reference |
| 8 | P5 · Still Pond / Mizu | Water element nature reference |
| 9 | P7 · Hearth / Hi | Fire element nature reference |
| 10 | P10 · Dawn Mountains | Session-complete emotional resolution |

**After all 10:** favourite the best generation from each → add to your Nendō moodboard → then proceed to the 10 production prompts from the rollout plan.
